About Laser Vision

Laser Vision is a project of Philly Bike Action!, an all-volunteer advocacy organization and registered 501(c)(4) nonprofit on a mission to expand Philly's protected bike lane network and improve the experience of riding a bike in Philadelphia.

Since our founding in April 2023, we have mobilized a grassroots movement of civic engagement and direct action demanding safer designs for our city streets.

Donations to Philly Bike Action help us promote safer and more effective bicycle infrastructure for the people of Philadelphia. We believe everyone deserves to be able to get around the city safely by bicycle, regardless of ability or age, and nobody deserves to be harassed or threatened.

Your donation will help us cover recurring costs like meeting room rentals and tech services (like this!), printing costs, and event materials.
